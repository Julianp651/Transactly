Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to your use of both the Transactly website (transactly.us) and the Transactly app.

Information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, share, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties.

We take reasonable steps to protect any information you submit. For the app, your data remains on your device unless you choose to back it up through an external service.
