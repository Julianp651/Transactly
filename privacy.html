Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2025

Transactly (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Currently, Transactly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data off your device. All your financial data is stored locally on your device and is never shared with us or third parties.

2. Cloud Services

In the future, Transactly may introduce optional cloud features, such as syncing your data across devices or cloud backup. These features may use third-party services such as Firebase. If/when this is added, you will be notified and given control over what data is synced.

3. Subscription Features

If Transactly offers subscription features in the future, payments will be processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

Transactly does not use analytics or crash reporting services at this time. If this changes, you will be informed and given options to opt in or out where required.

5. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or resources (such as support or privacy policies). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Transactly does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this page, and the effective date will be updated.
